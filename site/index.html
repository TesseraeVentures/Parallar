PLATE 02In one minute
— no jargon

A bond is a promise to pay. Parallar is what happens when it doesn’t.

Here is the entire product, in four sentences a layperson can check.

A company borrows from many people at once.

That’s a bond. On Stellar, the bond is digital, and its scheduled interest payments — coupons — are real transfers everyone can see, made to every single bondholder.

tokenized issuance · coupons as genuine on-ledger payments to N holders

Bondholders buy protection. Underwriters fund it — in advance.

Before any protection is sold, underwriters deposit real money into a shared reserve. Parallar will never promise a payout it isn’t already holding. How much protection each holder bought stays private — sealed, like a bid in an envelope.

fully-funded vault · positions as Poseidon commitments

If a payment is missed, mathematics notices.

Anyone can run Parallar’s settlement program. It reads the public payment record, confirms who wasn’t paid, opens each sealed position privately, and calculates every payout by a formula published in advance. Then it produces a compact mathematical certificate that the whole calculation was done exactly right.

RISC Zero zkVM · one Groth16 proof for determination + allocation

Stellar checks the certificate. The reserve pays.

The blockchain verifies the certificate in seconds and releases each payout from the reserve. There is no other way to move that money. No claims department, no determination committee, no administrator override — by construction, not by policy.

on-chain verification is the sole authorization path

Why “Parallar”

Astronomers measure a star’s true distance by parallax — observing it from two positions and trusting the displacement, not anyone’s word. Parallar settles financial promises the same way: truth by measurement, not by trust. Parallax × Stellar.

RULE 1 — FUNDED FIRST

Money in before promises out

Underwriters deposit reserves to earn premiums. Total protection sold can never exceed total reserves — checked the moment anyone tries to buy.

RULE 2 — UNTOUCHABLE

The reserve can’t be pulled

Reserves are held only in assets no issuer can claw back or freeze — Parallar’s factory refuses anything else at deployment. And while a payment deadline is being judged, underwriter withdrawals are locked.

RULE 3 — ONE EXIT

Only a proof moves money

The single path out of the reserve to protection holders is a verified settlement certificate. Not us, not an admin key, not a court order to a claims department. If the bond paid on time, that certificate cannot exist.

Plate 04 — The method

One default, measured four ways down.

01 · Observe

Read the public record

A coupon means paying every bondholder. After the deadline, anyone gathers the ledger’s payment record — counting honestly: payments later clawed back by the issuer don’t count.

02 · Determine

Run the published rules

The settlement program finds who was shorted, opens each sealed position privately, and computes payouts in proportion to the damage.

03 · Prove

Compress it to a certificate

The entire calculation becomes one compact proof. Only the exact published rules can produce a valid one.

04 · Settle

Verify on Stellar. Pay from the reserve.

The contract checks the certificate and releases the payouts. No other path exists.

PLATE 07Trust model
— stated plainly

Each certificate guarantees the settlement calculation followed the published rules — exactly, completely, over the sealed positions. Within that boundary, no party, including us, can inflate, favor, omit, or fabricate a payout.

It does not yet guarantee that the payment record fed into the calculation is complete. Anyone can settle with the true record, and deadlines are checked independently on-chain — but closing this gap fully means attested data feeds, and that work is named, scheduled, and published.

We state this because the institutions this is built for will ask — and because the honest answer is engineering, not hand-waving. The full register of what stands between this demonstration and a live issuance — data attestation, buyer-held keys, independent audit — is public in the repo.

PLATE 08Why Stellar

Built where real money already moves.

Stellar settles stablecoins, remittances, and tokenized bonds for institutions today — and its recent upgrades quietly added exactly the cryptography a settlement layer needs.

FINAL IN ~5 SECONDS

Stellar transactions are irreversible the moment they settle. A payment deadline is a crisp fact, not a probability — there is no “wait for confirmations” and no rollback, ever.

PROOF-NATIVE

Protocols 25–26 added native proof-verification machinery (BN254, Poseidon) so checking a settlement certificate on-chain is fast and cheap enough to gate every payout.

ISSUER-POWER AWARE

Regulated assets on Stellar give issuers real powers — clawback, freezing. Parallar’s rules account for them: clawed-back coupons count as default, frozen holders count as shorted, and reserves must be held in assets nobody can touch.
