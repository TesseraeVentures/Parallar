Trust model

Each proof guarantees the settlement computation — the version-pinned rules over the supplied inputs and committed positions — is correct. It does not guarantee the inputs are canonical: correct computation on inputs, not fair inputs. Within the proof boundary no party, including the operator, can inflate, favor, omit, or fabricate a payout. Attested feeds (gap G1) close the input-canonicity gap in production.
