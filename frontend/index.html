Paraar Live on Stellar Testnet ·
Default protection · settled by proof

When a bond skips a payment, the people it owes get paid.

Parallar is default protection for tokenized bonds. If a bond misses an interest payment, the holders who bought protection are paid from a reserve that was funded in advance — and the payout is decided by a mathematical proof, not a claims department, a committee, or an admin key.

It runs on Stellar. The amount of protection each holder bought stays private. Anyone can check the proof. And no one — not even us — can fake a payout, inflate one, or refuse one.

01How it
works

Four steps, and no one to trust in the middle.

A bond is a company borrowing from many people at once. Parallar protects the people it owes — and settles that protection by measurement, not by anyone's word.

A bond pays interest to many holders

On Stellar the bond is digital, and its scheduled interest payments — coupons — are real transfers anyone can see, made to every holder.

Buyers purchase protection — the reserve is funded first

Underwriters deposit real money into a shared reserve before any protection is sold; Parallar never promises a payout it isn't already holding. How much each buyer bought stays sealed — like a bid in an envelope.

If a payment is missed, the math notices

Anyone can run the settlement program. It reads the public payment record, confirms who wasn't paid, opens each sealed position privately, and computes every payout by a formula published in advance — then produces a compact proof that the whole calculation was done exactly right.

Stellar checks the proof — and the reserve pays

The blockchain verifies the proof and releases each payout. There is no other way to move that money — no claims department, no override. And if the bond paid on time, a valid proof cannot exist.

03Why you
can trust it

The reserve has exactly one way out.

Funded first

Money in before promises out

Total protection sold can never exceed the reserve — checked the moment anyone buys.

Untouchable

The reserve can't be pulled

Held only in assets no issuer can claw back or freeze; the factory refuses anything else.

One exit

Only a proof moves money

The single path out is a verified proof. Not us, not an admin key. No proof, no payout.

The proof guarantees the computation is correct — the published rules, run over the supplied records, with no party able to inflate, favour, omit, or fabricate a payout. It does not by itself guarantee the records are complete; making the input data itself tamper-proof (attested feeds) is the next step on the way to a live issuance. Truth by measurement, not by trust.
